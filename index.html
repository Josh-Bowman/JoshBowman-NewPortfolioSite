About Me

Despite having majored in music, my career path has revolved around computers, diagnostics, and technical support. Before the quarantines, I was working part-time as a Student Technician for the Citrus College Tech department. While there, I learned a great deal about diagnostics, troubleshooting, networks, and protocols.

Presently, I still receive work as a contracted computer technician. Here, I can apply my diagnostic skills while still learning new ways to fix things. Occasionally, I receive calls to visit customers in their homes to set-up or upgrade their existing systems, including not just their computers, but their home networks as well. Because of my 3+ years in this field, I've acquired a keen eye for detail and troubleshooting, when it comes to diagnosing issues and debugging.

Through Thinkful, I have an opportunity to acquire new skills in software engineering that is still related to my present talents. Even after learning full-stack development, I'm exited to expand my skill set and gain mastery over loosely or seemingly unrelated topics, so that I may have the skills to combine them for even greater projects!

I'm always looking forward to working with people of other trades and talents, as there is always a way to learn from them, and apply their knowledge to my own endeavors.
